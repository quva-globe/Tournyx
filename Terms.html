1. Fair Play & Conduct

Tournyx is dedicated to providing a highly competitive, yet totally fair gaming environment. By using this platform, you agree to abide by the following:

  • Zero Tolerance for Cheating: The use of aimbots, wallhacks, third-party scripts, or exploit bugs will result in an immediate, permanent ban.

  • Account Sharing: Players must use their own registered In-Game Name (IGN) and UID. Playing on someone else's account is prohibited.
2. Prizes & Wallet Points

Winning tournaments and completing offers rewards you with Tournyx Points (PTS).

  • Points are non-transferable between accounts.
  • Withdrawal requests are typically processed within 24-48 hours.
  • Tournyx reserves the right to withhold prizes if a player is found guilty of violating Fair Play rules during a match.
3. Privacy & Data Security
